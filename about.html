John Pinano | Owner

John has been climbing for more than 10 years. He was born in the Marshall Islands. He moved to the US to attend high school in Daly City, CA. After graduating college, he moved around to different cities for a few years before he settled down in Monterey County where he discovered rock climbing. After 10 years of climbing, he decided to open a rock gym to provide the local climbing community a place to call home.
